Selected Work
AICA Project Workshop

In the final semester of my bachelor’s degree, I took part in the AICA project workshop — an interdisciplinary initiative led by multiple Munich universities to address challenges at the intersection of AI, culture, and the arts. In a team of four, we developed an interactive art installation exploring feedback loops in generative AI models, paying homage to a musical piece by Alvin Lucier. I was mainly responsible for the technical development, from setting up a local StableDiffusion model and designing prompts to writing the control script and building the hardware interface using an ESP32. Despite the tight timeframe, this project showed me how rewarding it is to collaborate with people from different disciplines - and unexpectedly, it also taught me a lot about woodworking. Find the GitHub repo here.

Bachelor Thesis

As a follow-up to my work in the Solutions Department at ARRI, my bachelor’s thesis also focused on color calibration for Virtual Production studios. Specifically, I designed and implemented a universal calibration workflow that supports a wider range of camera models while integrating into the existing application. Next to the calibration algorithm, I developed a verification workflow, designed an accompanying user interface, and conducted extensive testing across diverse camera models and production conditions to ensure robustness and accuracy. This project gave me the opportunity to explore the theoretical and mathematical fundamentals of color science and digital imaging, which was especially interesting for me, coming from an artistic background. Find the entire thesis here.

Träumen

Träumen is an unfinished photography project about the fascination I have with memory and dream images. The original idea was to visualize these concepts using photography, capturing everyday moments that evoke a sense of surrealism, as if they were part of a dream. On a less metaphorical level, it became a way for me to disconnect during stressful times - grabbing my camera and following wherever my intuition led me. The plan is to eventually make a printed zine out of these, I just haven’t found the time to do it. Find the full set of images here.
